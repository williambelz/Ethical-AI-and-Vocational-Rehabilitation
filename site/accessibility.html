WCAG Compliance

All resources should meet at least WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Use proper color contrast, semantic headings, and keyboard-friendly navigation.

Document & Media Accessibility

Images require descriptive alt text, videos should have captions, and PDFs must be tagged for screen reader compatibility.

Inclusive Design

Consider neurodivergent users and those who rely on assistive technology. Avoid flashing content and provide plain-language summaries where possible.
