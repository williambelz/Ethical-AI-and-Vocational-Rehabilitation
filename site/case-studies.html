Government & Agency Pilots

Some state agencies have tested AI-driven tools to streamline vocational assessments. Measuring placement rates and user satisfaction helps evaluate success.

Academic & Nonprofit Projects

Universities and nonprofits explore computer-vision simulation, job matching, and other innovations. Ethical safeguards include clear consent and oversight.

Private Sector Efforts

Companies may offer chatbots or automated screening. They must provide transparency and allow users to reach human counselors when needed.

Lessons Learned

Past failures highlight the importance of diverse training data and accessible interfaces. Communities affected by AI should be involved from the start.
