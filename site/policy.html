U.S. Regulations

Key laws such as the Americans with Disabilities Act and the Rehabilitation Act guide how technology must support accessibility. HIPAA and privacy rules also apply when handling health records.

Section 508 standards for federal agencies specifically require that automated decision systems remain accessible. Agencies should integrate accessibility reviews into procurement and testing cycles.

International Frameworks

Regulations like the GDPR and Canada's Accessible Canada Act emphasize data protection and user rights. Following these helps ensure global compliance.

Templates & Checklists

Collaborative initiatives like the OECD's working group on AI offer guidance for harmonizing standards internationally. VR programs can leverage these resources when serving clients across borders.

Organizations benefit from policy templates covering AI governance, data sharing, and privacy impact assessments.
